## Executive Summary

**VSOM** (Vision, Strategy, Objectives, Metrics) is a comprehensive ontological framework for describing an organization's strategic architecture through four interconnected components that cascade across **ALL 20 modern C-Suite roles**. This framework provides a structured approach to represent both Corporate and Functional Strategies, cascading through OKR (Objectives and Key Results) systems to drive measurable progress across specified temporal timeframes.

## Governance Framework - Complete RACI/RBAC

### ROLES Hierarchy - Complete Structure
**Board Level**: Board of Directors, Audit Committee, Compensation Committee, Risk Committee
**Executive Level**: CEO (ultimate accountability for corporate performance)
**C-Suite Level**: All 20 C-Suite roles (functional accountability within domains)
**Senior Management**: VPs, Senior Directors (departmental accountability)
**Middle Management**: Directors, Managers (team accountability)
**Individual Level**: Individual contributors (task accountability)

### RACI Application - Complete Framework
**Strategic Planning**:
- **Responsible**: Strategy teams across all functional areas, led by CSO (Strategy)
- **Accountable**: CEO (ultimate corporate accountability), each C-Suite leader (functional accountability)
- **Consulted**: Cross-functional stakeholders, subject matter experts, external advisors
- **Informed**: All employees, shareholders, key stakeholders, regulatory bodies

**Functional Execution**:
- **Responsible**: Functional teams under each of the 20 C-Suite leaders
- **Accountable**: Each C-Suite leader for their functional domain
- **Consulted**: Cross-functional partners, internal customers, subject matter experts
- **Informed**: Executive team, relevant stakeholders, governance committees

### RBAC Implementation - Complete Access Control
**Strategic Level**: CEO + CSO (Strategy) - Full strategic access across all functions
**Functional Level**: Each C-Suite role - Full functional access, read access to integrated functions
**Cross-Functional Level**: Collaboration matrices enabling appropriate access across functional boundaries
**Departmental Level**: VPs, Directors - Full departmental access, functional summary access
**Team Level**: Managers - Team access, departmental summary access
**Individual Level**: Contributors - Individual access, team summary access
