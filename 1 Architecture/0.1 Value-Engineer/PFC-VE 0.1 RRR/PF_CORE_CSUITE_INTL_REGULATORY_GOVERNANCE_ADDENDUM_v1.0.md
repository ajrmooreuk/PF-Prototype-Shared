## 1. Purpose & Scope

This addendum extends the C-Suite Roles Framework to provide comprehensive coverage of international regulatory governance across the primary meat trading jurisdictions: United Kingdom, Australia, European Union, and key international frameworks. It defines jurisdiction-specific regulatory bodies, compliance requirements, certification regimes, and the C-Suite accountability matrix for multi-jurisdictional operations.

### 1.1 Jurisdictional Coverage

| Region | Primary Markets | Key Trade Flows |
|--------|----------------|-----------------|
| **United Kingdom** | Domestic, EU export, Middle East, Asia | Lamb, beef export; poultry import |
| **Australia** | Asia-Pacific, Middle East, USA, EU | Beef, lamb, live export |
| **European Union** | Intra-EU, UK, Switzerland, Asia | Pork, beef, poultry |
| **Americas** | USA, Brazil, Argentina, Canada | Beef, pork, poultry |
| **Asia-Pacific** | China, Japan, Korea, SE Asia | Import destinations |
| **Middle East** | GCC, Levant | Halal import destinations |

## 5. International & Supranational Frameworks

### 5.1 World Trade Organization (WTO)

#### 5.1.1 SPS Agreement

The Agreement on the Application of Sanitary and Phytosanitary Measures governs food safety and animal/plant health measures in international trade.

| Principle | Description | C-Suite Implication |
|-----------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Harmonization** | Measures based on international standards | Align to Codex/WOAH standards |
| **Equivalence** | Accept different measures achieving same protection | Negotiate equivalence agreements |
| **Risk Assessment** | Science-based measures | Support risk assessment processes |
| **Regionalization** | Disease-free zone recognition | Maintain zone status |
| **Transparency** | Notification of measures | Monitor SPS notifications |
| **Non-Discrimination** | MFN and National Treatment | Ensure fair treatment |

#### 5.1.2 TBT Agreement

Technical Barriers to Trade Agreement governs non-SPS technical regulations.

| Element | Relevance to Meat Trade |
|---------|------------------------|
| Technical Regulations | Labelling, packaging, composition |
| Standards | Voluntary standards recognition |
| Conformity Assessment | Testing, certification procedures |

### 5.2 Codex Alimentarius Commission

| Standard Type | Examples | Application |
|--------------|----------|-------------|
| **Commodity Standards** | Canned corned beef, meat products | Product specifications |
| **General Standards** | Food hygiene, labelling | Cross-cutting requirements |
| **Codes of Practice** | Meat hygiene, HACCP | Operational guidance |
| **Guidelines** | Risk assessment, equivalence | Regulatory guidance |
| **MRLs** | Veterinary drug residues | Residue limits |

### 5.3 World Organisation for Animal Health (WOAH, formerly OIE)

| Code | Scope | Key Standards |
|------|-------|---------------|
| **Terrestrial Animal Health Code** | Live animals, products | Disease status, import requirements |
| **Aquatic Animal Health Code** | Fish, shellfish | Aquatic disease management |
| **Manual of Diagnostic Tests** | Laboratory methods | Testing protocols |

#### Disease Status Implications

| Disease | Trade Implication | C-Suite Accountability |
|---------|-------------------|----------------------|
| **FMD (Foot & Mouth Disease)** | Market access fundamental | CEO (strategic), CCO (compliance) |
| **BSE** | Negligible/controlled risk status | CQO (surveillance), CCO (status) |
| **ASF (African Swine Fever)** | Pork trade exclusion | CSCO (sourcing), CCO (compliance) |
| **AI (Avian Influenza)** | Poultry trade restrictions | CCO (monitoring), CEO (crisis) |

### 8.2 Global vs. Regional Accountability Split

| Function | Global (C-Suite) | Regional |
|----------|-----------------|----------|
| **Regulatory Strategy** | CCO sets policy | Regional Reg Dir implements |
| **Quality Standards** | CQO sets minimum standards | Regional QA Dir ensures compliance + local requirements |
| **Export Certification** | CCO oversees program | Regional Reg Dir obtains certificates |
| **Customer Relationships** | CRO strategic accounts | Regional Sales for local accounts |
| **Supplier Approval** | CQO/CSCO approve criteria | Regional procurement manages suppliers |
| **Crisis Response** | CEO leads, CCO/CQO coordinate | Regional MD first response |
